Once upon a time there was little fish in the middle of the endless ocean.

Every night she was dreaming about exploring word outside of the ocean.

She dont want to give up and spent countless hours to find the way make her dream happend.

After so many hours of hard work she was first fish which invented mechanical wings and now she was ready...
